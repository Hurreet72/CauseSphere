Who We Are?

CauseSphere is an innovative, AI-integrated crowdfunding platform designed to connect donors and beneficiaries securely. We focus on transparency, accountability, and impact, ensuring that every donation reaches the right hands with real-time tracking and verification.

What We Offer?

  • Strict Verification & AI Review:

    Prevents fraud and ensures authenticity.

  • Direct Hospital & NGO Fund Transfer:

    Eliminates intermediaries for secure transactions.

  • Real-Time Fund Utilization Dashboard:

    .Provides full transparency on how donations are used.

  • Milestone Achievements with Certificates & Badges:

    Rewards donors for their contributions.

Our Mission

To revolutionize online giving by creating a trustworthy and sustainable crowdfunding ecosystem that empowers individuals, communities, and NGOs.
