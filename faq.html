Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about CauseSphere.

General Questions

  • What is CauseSphere?

    CauseSphere is an AI-integrated crowdfunding platform designed to connect donors and beneficiaries securely while ensuring transparency.

  • How does CauseSphere ensure transparency?

    We provide real-time fund tracking, AI-driven verification, and direct fund transfers to hospitals and NGOs.

Donation & Fund Management

  • How can I donate?

    You can donate through our secure online platform using multiple payment options.

  • Where does my donation go?

    All donations go directly to verified hospitals and NGOs without intermediaries.

  • Can I track how my donation is used?

    Yes! Our real-time fund utilization dashboard allows you to see exactly how your donations are making an impact.

Security & Verification

  • How does CauseSphere prevent fraud?

    Our AI-driven review system and strict verification processes ensure all fundraising campaigns are legitimate.

  • Are my payment details secure?

    Yes, we use encrypted transactions to ensure your payment details remain safe.
