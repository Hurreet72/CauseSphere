What We Offer

At CauseSphere, we provide a range of services to ensure seamless and secure crowdfunding. Our AI-driven platform enhances transparency, security, and efficiency in online fundraising.

  • Strict Verification & AI Review

    We implement rigorous verification processes to prevent fraud and ensure the authenticity of campaigns.

  • Direct Hospital & NGO Fund Transfer

    Funds are transferred directly to hospitals and NGOs, eliminating intermediaries for secure transactions.

  • Real-Time Fund Utilization Dashboard

    Donors can track how their contributions are being utilized in real time, ensuring complete transparency.

  • Milestone Achievements with Certificates & Badges

    We reward donors for their generosity with milestone certificates and badges.

  • Community Engagement & Awareness

    We actively engage with communities to spread awareness about important causes and drive impact-driven initiatives.
